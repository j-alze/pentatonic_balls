Before you start, you can choose between different presets. They are all carefully balanced to give you the best experience, except for "Dev Mode", which is thought to use when developing new presets. When you load the game, you'll need to place the five balls without placing them too close to each other and the walls. If you choose one of the last two gravity types, you'll be able to control the strength and direction of the gravity with your mouse pointer.
Enjoy the music!
